About model

Experience the ultimate combination of speed and torque with the Makita 18V LXT® Lithium-Ion Cordless 1/2-Inch Hammer Driver-Drill. This compact powerhouse is equipped with a highly efficient 2.0Ah battery, capable of achieving a full charge in an impressive 25 minutes.

Its versatility knows no bounds, as it effortlessly transitions between functioning as a driver-drill or a hammer driver-drill, catering to a wide range of applications in wood, metal, masonry, and concrete.

Star Protection Computer Controls™

For optimal performance in industrial settings, Makita cordless tools are meticulously crafted. To ensure utmost safety during demanding tasks, Makita introduces the cutting-edge STAR Protection Computer Control™. This innovative technology serves as a communication system, constantly monitoring usage conditions and facilitating data exchange between the tool and battery.

When you attach a Makita 18V LXT® battery featuring the STAR symbol to a Makita tool adorned with either the STAR symbol or a yellow terminal, you can take full advantage of the benefits offered by the advanced STAR Protection Computer Controls™.

Its primary objective is to safeguard against overloading, overdischarging, and overheating. Should STAR detect any potential risks of overload, overdischarge, or overheating to either the tool or battery, it promptly triggers an automatic shutdown. At this point, the user has the option to restart the tool, replace or recharge the battery, and resume their work with the tool.
